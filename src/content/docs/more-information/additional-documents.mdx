## Government Documents

These documents are published by official government agencies and provide standards, guidelines, and requirements for cybersecurity and information assurance.

|:---------|:------|

| [NCP Tahoe Checklist](https://ncp.nist.gov/checklist/1301) | mSCP Guidance for macOS 26 (Tahoe) |
| [NIST SP 800-63](https://www.nist.gov/itl/tig/projects/special-publication-800-63) | Digital Identity Guidelines |
| [NIST SP 800-171 Rev 3](https://csrc.nist.gov/pubs/sp/800/171/r3/final) | Protecting Controlled Unclassified Information in Nonfederal Systems |
| [NIST SP 800-219 Rev 1](https://csrc.nist.gov/pubs/sp/800/219/r1/final) | macOS Security Compliance Project (mSCP) |
